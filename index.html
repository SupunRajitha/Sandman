Sandman

an application built with late night developers in mind

  • Sleep Notification: notfies know when optimal rest times are generated by the algorithm and reccommends a shutdown.
  • Uptime Notifications: notifies you when your computers uptime is over 12 hours and reccomends a restart.
  • Out of sight, out of mind: lives in the task bar the beauty of Sandman is its ability to run in the background.
  • Open source: find a bug? Make an issue.
